About 77

Built for people who
treat fitness like a career.

77 Fitness Studio is a premium fitness destination in Chennai that blends fitness, lifestyle, and community. From curated programs and world-class machines to post-workout recovery and work-friendly zones — every detail is designed for consistency, safety, and long-term progress.

  • Thoughtful layouts that separate intense training, group classes, and calm recovery.
  • Clean, well-lit interiors with a focus on safety, hygiene, and comfort.
  • Community that keeps you accountable without ever feeling crowded or chaotic.
  • Short, effective sessions designed to fit around work and family schedules.
